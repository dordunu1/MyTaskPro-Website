Privacy Policy

At MyTaskPro, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our app.

Google User Data Access

MyTaskPro requires access to certain Google services to function properly. Specifically, we access:

  • Google Calendar: To see, edit, share, and manage calendar events that represent your tasks
  • Basic Profile Information: To associate you with your personal information on Google
  • Email Address: To see your primary Google Account email address for account identification

Information We Collect

  • Task Data: Your tasks are stored directly in your Google Calendar as events. We do not maintain a separate database of your tasks.
  • Google Account Information: We only use this for authentication and do not store any Google account credentials.

Data Storage and Security

  • All task data is stored directly in your Google Calendar - we do not maintain separate servers or databases.
  • We do not collect or store any Google user data on our servers.
  • Your data remains within your Google Account and is protected by Google's security measures.

Data Sharing and Transfer

MyTaskPro does not share, transfer, or disclose your Google user data to any third parties. All data interactions occur directly between the app and your Google Calendar.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Since all data is stored in your Google Calendar, you maintain full control over your data.
  • You can delete your data at any time by:
    • Deleting individual tasks/events from your Google Calendar
    • Revoking MyTaskPro's access through your Google Account settings
  • When you delete a task, it is immediately and permanently removed from your Google Calendar.

Your Choices

  • You can revoke MyTaskPro's access to your Google Account at any time through your Google Account settings.
  • You can delete individual tasks directly from the app or Google Calendar.
  • You maintain full control over your Google Calendar data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
