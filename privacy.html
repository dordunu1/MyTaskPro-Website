Privacy Policy

At MyTaskPro, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Task Data: We collect and store the tasks you create, including titles, descriptions, due dates, and completion status.
  • Google Account Information: If you choose to sync your tasks, we access your Google account information for authentication purposes.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous data about how you use the app to improve our services.
  • Camera Data: When you use the camera feature within the app, we temporarily access your device's camera to capture images. These images are stored locally on your device and/or in your Google account if sync is enabled.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the MyTaskPro service.
  • To sync your tasks across devices when you enable Google Sync.
  • To improve and personalize your experience with the app.
  • To communicate with you about app updates or respond to your inquiries.
  • To enable the capture and storage of images related to your tasks when you use the camera feature.

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: By default, your tasks and any images captured within the app are stored locally on your device.
  • Cloud Storage: If you enable Google Sync, your tasks and associated images are also stored securely in your Google account.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

Camera Usage

  • The app requests access to your device's camera only when you choose to use the camera feature within the app.
  • Images captured using the app's camera feature are used solely for task-related purposes within the app and are not shared with third parties.
  • You can revoke camera permissions at any time through your device settings.

Your Choices

  • You can use MyTaskPro without creating an account or syncing to Google.
  • You can enable or disable Google Sync at any time in the app settings.
  • You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.
  • You can choose whether to grant or revoke camera permissions for the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
